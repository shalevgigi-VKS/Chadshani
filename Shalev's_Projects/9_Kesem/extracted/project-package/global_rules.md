# Global Rules — קסם הידיעה

## Language
- All episode content: Hebrew only
- Narration: clear, slow, warm Hebrew — no slang
- Text on screen (if any): Hebrew only

## Duration
- Every episode: 40–50 seconds. No exceptions.
- If script runs long: cut content, do not speed up narration

## Audience
- Primary: ages 4–7 (simple words, short sentences, repetition)
- Secondary: ages 8–10 (slightly more detail tolerated but not required)
- Default to 4–7 when in doubt

## Characters
- Always: one child character + one animal companion
- Animal speaks or reacts (not silent prop)
- Both characters are present in every episode
- Characters do not change mid-episode

## Tone
- Tone is chosen per episode (calm / energetic / playful-smart)
- Tone must be consistent within a single episode — no mixing
- Never scary, never urgent, never negative-consequence-focused

## Pacing
- Narration: slow and deliberate — pause between sentences
- No more than 4 sentences of narration per episode
- Each sentence: max 10 words

## Ending
- Every episode ends with one positive reinforcement line
- Child character demonstrates the habit visually at the end

## No-go zones
- No frightening imagery (bacteria shown as cute, not gross)
- No punishment framing ("if you don't, you'll get sick")
- No adult humor
- No violence, even cartoon
