# Module 07 — Episode 01: Teeth Brushing

## Topic
צחצוח שיניים — Why do we brush our teeth?

## Key Message
"שיניים נקיות — חיוך מאושר!" (8 words — fits rule)

## Script

ACT 1 — THE QUESTION (6 sec)
[Child looks at toothbrush, puzzled expression]
Child: "[Animal name], למה צריך לצחצח שיניים כל יום?"

ACT 2 — THE EXPLANATION (30 sec)
[Animal smiles, leans in warmly]
Animal: "כי אוכל נשאר בין השיניים אחרי כל ארוחה."
[Visual: cute cartoon food particles between teeth, smiling]

Animal: "ואם לא מנקים — הם יכולים להכאיב."
[Visual: a tiny cartoon tooth looking sad, then happy after brushing]

Animal: "שיניים נקיות — חיוך מאושר!" [KEY MESSAGE]
[Visual: bright, happy smile]

ACT 3 — THE MAGIC MOMENT (10 sec)
[Child brushes teeth enthusiastically]
[Child turns to camera with big smile]
Animal: "יש לך חיוך של קוסם!"
[Upbeat music sting]
[Text on screen: "שיניים נקיות — חיוך מאושר!"]

## Scene Breakdown

| Scene | Act | Visual | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| 01 | 1 | Child + toothbrush, puzzled | 6 sec |
| 02 | 2 | Cute food between cartoon teeth | 10 sec |
| 03 | 2 | Sad tooth → happy tooth | 12 sec |
| 04 | 2 | Key message delivery | 8 sec |
| 05 | 3 | Child brushing + big smile | 10 sec |

## Voiceover Notes
- Child voice: curious, slightly dramatic on the question
- Animal voice: warm, enthusiastic, not rushed
- Pause 1 second between Act 1 and Act 2
- Key message in Act 2: slight emphasis, warm smile energy
- Music sting enters exactly at Act 3 smile moment

## Production Notes
- Toothbrush color: bright turquoise (memorable visual)
- Food particles: cartoon pizza slice, apple, friendly-looking
- Tooth character (Act 2): small, white, round, expressive eyes
- Background Act 1: bathroom tiles (warm cream color)
- Background Act 2: white clean space with light background
- Background Act 3: same bathroom, bright and warm

## Acceptance Criteria
- Total duration: 40–50 seconds when narrated at slow pace
- Key message appears twice (spoken + on screen)
- Child demonstrates habit at end
- No scary imagery (bacteria = not shown, just "food particles")
- Child under 6 understands message after one viewing
