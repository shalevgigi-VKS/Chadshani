# Module 02 — Episode Template

## Objective
Define the reusable 3-act script and scene structure for every episode.

## Timing Breakdown
Total: 40–50 seconds

| Act | Name | Duration | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | The Question | 5–8 sec | Child asks "why do we [habit]?" |
| 2 | The Explanation | 25–35 sec | Animal explains, visually shown |
| 3 | The Magic Moment | 8–10 sec | Child does habit, feels great |

## Scene Count
- Act 1: 1 scene
- Act 2: 2–3 scenes maximum
- Act 3: 1 scene

## Script Template

ACT 1 — THE QUESTION
[Child character name] looks at [habit object] and asks:
"[Animal name], למה צריך [לעשות את ההרגל]?"

ACT 2 — THE EXPLANATION
[Animal name] smiles and says:
"כי [reason 1 — max 8 words]!"
[Visual: show reason 1]

"וגם, [reason 2 — max 8 words]."
[Visual: show reason 2]

KEY MESSAGE (said clearly):
"[One sentence, max 8 words, repeated at end]"

ACT 3 — THE MAGIC MOMENT
[Child character name] [does the habit].
[Child character name] smiles.
[Animal name] says: "[Positive reinforcement line — max 6 words]!"
[Upbeat music sting]

## Word Count Rule
- Max 4 narration sentences per episode
- Each sentence: max 10 words
- Read entire script aloud before finalizing — must fit in 40–50 sec at slow pace

## Key Message Rule
- Defined before writing the script
- Must appear in Act 2 and again in Act 3 (slight variation allowed)
- Must be memorable — a child should repeat it after one viewing
