# Business Rules — קסם הידיעה

## Episode Structure (always 3 acts)
Act 1 — The Question (5–8 sec): Child character asks "why do we [habit]?"
Act 2 — The Explanation (25–35 sec): Animal companion explains, visually demonstrated
Act 3 — The Magic Moment (8–10 sec): Child does the habit, feels great, positive line

## Character Rule
- Child character = the asker (curious, relatable)
- Animal companion = the knower (warm, never condescending)
- Animal explains with enthusiasm, never lectures

## Topic Selection Rule
- One topic per episode only
- Topic must be a repeatable daily/weekly habit
- Topic must be physically demonstrable visually

## Voiceover Rule
- One narrator voice OR two voices (child + animal)
- If two voices: child voice higher/younger, animal voice warm and calm
- Hebrew diction: clear consonants, no rushing

## Visual Rule
- Format chosen per episode: animation / static+voiceover / animation+real voice
- Background: always bright, warm colors (no dark backgrounds)
- Characters: always fully visible, not cropped

## Repetition Rule
- Key message repeated twice per episode (once in act 2, once in act 3)
- Key message = one sentence, max 8 words

## Music Rule
- Background music: soft children's instrumental, plays automatically under TTS voiceover
- Volume: significantly lower than voiceover — supports mood, never competes
- Timing: starts with animation, fades after last scene
- Style: warm, playful, encouraging — no lyrics, no dramatic swings

## Episode Naming Convention
- Format: "קסם הידיעה — [topic in one word or short phrase]"
- Example: "קסם הידיעה — צחצוח שיניים"
